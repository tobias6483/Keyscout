# Code of Conduct

KeyScout should be a welcoming project for people who care about macOS productivity, native tools, and open-source software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially when discussing edge cases or platform limitations.
- Keep feedback focused on the project, code, design, or documentation.
- Make room for new contributors.
- Be clear when something is opinion, preference, or verified behavior.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated hostile or disruptive comments
- Spam or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block users when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

If you see a problem, contact the repository owner through GitHub.
